Merges several rows in one using specified separator.

Example: Given original dataset:

a b c d e f g h i j
Industry 18-20 18-20 20-35 20-35 35-50 35-50 50-65 50-65 Total
Men Woman Men Woman Men Woman Men Women
Agriculture 50 12 132 100 115 96 106 80 691

It is possible to merge rows 0 and 1 :

a b c d e f g h i j
Industry 18-20, Men 18-20, Women 20-35, Men 20-35, Women 35-50, Men 35-50, Women 50-65, Men 50-65, Women Total
Agriculture 50 12 132 100 115 96 106 80
