Changes a row position inside a dataset. It is possible to shift row either to the specified index or to the end of the dataset. Other rows will be shifted appropriately.

Example: Given original dataset containing records about orders made by clients:

firstName lastName orderNumber totalItems totalCost
Alice Smith 1111 5 150
Bob Johnson 857 7 70
Alice Smith 1112 30 340
Alice Williams 505 1 170
Bob Johnson 858 3 370
Mary Williams 1543 1 15

It is possible to shift a row number 1 to position 3:

firstName lastName orderNumber totalItems totalCost
Alice Smith 1111 5 150
Alice Smith 1112 30 340
Alice Williams 505 1 170
Bob Johnson 857 7 70
Bob Johnson 858 3 370
Mary Williams 1543 1 15

Parameters

Shift to index Shift to the end of dataset
