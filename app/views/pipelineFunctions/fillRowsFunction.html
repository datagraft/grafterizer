Fills empty cells in a row(rows). The cell values will be copied through the row over blank cells until the next non-blank cell

Example: Given original dataset:

a b c d e f g h i j
Industry Under 20 20-35 35-50 Over 50 Total
Men Woman Men Woman Men Woman Men Women
Agriculture 50 12 132 100 115 96 106 80 691

It is possible to fill in missing values in row 0 :

a b c d e f g h i j
Industry Under 20 Under 20 20-35 20-35 35-50 35-50 Over 50 Over 50 Total
Industry Men Women Men Women Men Women Men Women Total
Agriculture 50 12 132 100 115 96 106 80 691

Parameters
